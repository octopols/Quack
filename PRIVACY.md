# Privacy Policy for Quack

**Last Updated:** January 5, 2026

## Overview

Quack is a browser extension that allows users to search through YouTube comments and replies. This privacy policy explains how Quack handles user data.

## Data Collection

**Quack does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

### What Quack Stores Locally

Quack stores the following data **locally on your device only** using Chrome's storage API:

- **User Preferences:** Search settings such as case sensitivity, search in replies, and highlight matches options
- **Search History:** Your recent search queries (stored locally for convenience)

This data:
- Never leaves your browser
- Is not transmitted to any external servers
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

### What Quack Accesses

Quack accesses YouTube video pages to:
- Inject the search interface into the comments section
- Read comment data from YouTube's internal APIs to enable search functionality

Quack does NOT:
- Track your browsing history
- Collect any personally identifiable information
- Access any websites other than YouTube
- Use any analytics or tracking services

## Permissions Explained

- **storage:** Used to save your search preferences locally
- **youtube.com host permission:** Required to add the search interface to YouTube video pages

## Third-Party Services

Quack does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs.

## Children's Privacy

Quack does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/octopols/Quack).
